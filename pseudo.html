When the player clicks on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. 4. Your game will hide this amount until the player clicks a crystal. When they do click one, update the player's score counter. 5. The player wins if their total score matches the random number from the beginning of the game. Also, all the crystals will have four new hidden values. Of course, the user's score (and score counter) will reset to zero. 8. The app should show the number of games the player wins and loses.
